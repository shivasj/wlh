Who we are
The Women's League of Hartsville is a not-for-profit service and social club for all women who live or work in the Hartsville, SC area. Established in 1972, as a venue for women to share social, educational, and community service experiences, The Women's League of Hartsville is committed to increasing awareness and dedicating resources to enhance the community in which we live. In addition to monthly gatherings and ongoing volunteer work, the group hosts a Live and Silent Auction to benefit other deserving non-profits.
